# PRD Template

Use this template structure when creating Product Requirements Documents.

```markdown
# PRD: {Feature Name}

**Created**: {Date}
**Status**: Draft | Review | Approved
**Target Release**: {Version or date}

---

## Executive Summary

{2-3 sentence overview of what this is and why it matters}

---

## Problem Statement

### Current Situation
{What's the current state? What's painful or missing?}

### Impact
- **Users**: {How users are affected}
- **Business**: {How business is affected}

### Opportunity
{What becomes possible if we solve this?}

---

## Goals & Success Criteria

### Primary Goals
1. {Specific, measurable objective}
2. {Specific, measurable objective}

### Success Metrics
- **{Metric}**: {Baseline → Target}
- **{Metric}**: {Baseline → Target}

### Non-Goals
{What we explicitly won't do}

---

## User Personas & Use Cases

### Primary Persona: {Name}
- **Role**: {Job title/description}
- **Goals**: {What they want to achieve}
- **Pain Points**: {What frustrates them}

### Use Case 1: {Scenario Name}
**Goal**: {What user wants to accomplish}
**Steps**:
1. User {action}
2. System {response}
3. Success: {Outcome}

---

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements (Must Have)
1. **{Requirement}**: {Description}
   - Acceptance: {How we verify this works}

### Non-Functional Requirements
- **Performance**: {Response time, throughput}
- **Security**: {Auth, data protection}
- **Usability**: {Accessibility, browser support}

---

## Constraints

### Technical Constraints
- {System limitations}
- {Technology requirements}

### Business Constraints
- {Budget, timeline, resources}

---

## Out of Scope

{Things explicitly not included}

---

## Open Questions

- [ ] {Question requiring answer}
- [ ] {Uncertainty to resolve}
```
